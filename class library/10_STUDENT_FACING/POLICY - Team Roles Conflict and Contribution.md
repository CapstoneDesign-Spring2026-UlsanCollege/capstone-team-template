# POLICY - Team Roles, Conflict, and Contribution

## Purpose

Team problems should be handled with evidence, not drama.

This is not about blame. It is about visible ownership and evidence.

## Weekly Roles

- **PM:** standup, board, Sprint Packet submission.
- **Scribe:** decisions, risks, blockers, notes.
- **QA Lead:** checklist, testing, demo readiness.
- **Demo Driver:** weekly demo and backup plan.
- **AI Steward:** checks AI-assisted work for understanding and review.

## Minimum Contribution Standard

Each student must provide weekly receipts:

- 2–3 links,
- clear ownership,
- short summary,
- AI Use Note when relevant.

## Warning Signs

- missed receipts,
- vague claims,
- no linked PRs/Issues,
- cannot explain assigned work,
- repeated absence from evidence,
- peer flags,
- hidden blockers.
